I am an automation engineer from Italy.
Currently, I'm pursuing a Ph.D. at the University of Padua.

My main focus is on using both a theoretical approach and numerical simulations to study and engineer control laws for complex dynamical systems, expecially quantum systems.

Research

My research interests are in the field of control and systems theory.
Topics of interest include (but are not limited to):

  • Quantum informatio theory and quantum probability theory;
  • Quantum control;
  • Quantum random walks;
  • Open quantum systems;
  • Non-linear and complex systems;
  • Probability theory and hidden Markov models.
