Hi, my name is Tommaso Grigoletto.

I am an automation engineer and a Ph.D. student at the University of Padua.

My research interests are in the field of control system theory.
Topics of interest include: most importantly quantum information and control, quantum random walk and open quantum systems but also non-linear systems, complex systems, probability models, Hidden Markov Models.
